What is Kanban?

Kanban is a scheduling system for lean manufacturing. Our Join has three important areas.

Add Task

Here you can create a new task. The inputfields "Title", "Description" and "Due date" are obligatory. Under "Category" you can chose between IT, Sales, Marketing and Management. Also the "Priority" and the current "Status" is adjustable. By clicking on the "+" under the "ASSINGED TO" you are able to add colleagues or other persons who are responsible for this task. You can search by username or by mail. It is possible to dismiss persons from the list per drag and drop over the bin icon. By clicking on the "Create Task" Button your task will be pushed in the Backlog area.

Backlog

Here you have a list with all tasks which are created. There you can decide which task you want on your board. Click on the card to see the details of the card. In the detailed view you have on the bottom a button "Accept Task". After click on this button you will see your task on the board.

Board

Here you can change the status of your task according the working step. Drag the box with the task which you are working on from "TO DO" to "IN PROGRESS" or on the step you are.
